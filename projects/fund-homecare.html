Fund Homecare Canada

Built and launched the official website for a Canadian charity dedicated to providing homecare funding for palliative cancer patients in Ontario who are in financial need.

Role Web Developer
Type Charity Website
Status Live

Overview

Fund Homecare Canada is a registered charity that provides funding to assist homecare as a respite for palliative cancer patients in Ontario who are in financial need. I worked with a team to build and launch their official website, creating a digital presence for the organization.

Impact

The website serves as the primary digital touchpoint for the charity, enabling them to share their mission, accept donations, and connect with families in need across Ontario. It provides essential information about the organization's services and how patients can access support.

Key Features

  • Clean, accessible design that communicates the charity's mission
  • Information about services and eligibility for support
  • Donation and volunteer pathways
  • Responsive design for access across all devices
  • About section with the organization's story and team
