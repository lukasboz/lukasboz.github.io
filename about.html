My Journey

Building with purpose

My journey into software development started with a curiosity about how things work and a drive to create things people actually use. As a Computer Science student at Western University, I've channeled that curiosity into building practical applications that make a difference.

I've had the opportunity to work at J.D. Power, first as a QA Automation Engineer Intern and now as a Software Developer. This experience has taught me the value of writing clean, maintainable code and the importance of understanding the end user.

Outside of work, I'm passionate about building side projects that push my skills -- from real-time multiplayer platforms to AI-driven tools. I believe the difference between just building a thing and building something people actually use is massive. That philosophy drives everything I create.

My Process

How I approach development

A structured process that ensures quality from concept to launch.

01

Understand

Research the problem space, define requirements, and understand who the end user is and what they need.

02

Plan & Design

Architect the solution, plan the tech stack, and create wireframes or prototypes before writing code.

03

Build & Iterate

Develop incrementally with clean code practices, testing as I go and refining based on feedback.

04

Test & Refine

Thorough testing across scenarios and devices. Fix edge cases and optimize performance.

05

Ship & Support

Deploy the solution, gather user feedback, and iterate to ensure long-term success.
